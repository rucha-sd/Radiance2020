ABOUT US

PASC

ACM PICT is a student chapter organization subsidiary of Association for Computing Machinery, a worldwide learning society for computing. We always have the vision to give the best of our capabilities to help an individual to grow in all aspects and also to make ACM student chapter an ideal platform for students.

PASC-W

Encouraging gender equality and women empowerment, PASC-W promotes full engagement of women in the technical domain. Through its collaborations and initiatives, PASC-W provides a large range of services to all ACM members along with pushing the advancement of contribution of women in the field of computing

ACM-W

ACM-W supports, celebrates, and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field, providing a wide range of programs and services to ACM members and working in the larger community to advance the contributions of technical women.

CODE WARS

Code Wars is a competition where the participants are expected to code for given problemstatements.
Round 1 : The participants will have to write correct and efficient code for a given set of problem statements . This competition will be hosted on hackerrank.
Round 2 : The competition will take place in pair of teams who will compete with each other for 20 minutes. The team which loses will be eliminated from the competition. The top two teams will have a final round of 30 minutes and the team that succeeds in all the rounds will be the ultimate winner.
Rules and Regulations:
1. Both the groups, FE-SE and TE-BE, will be given 2 hours to complete the first round.
2. The participants can code in either C, C++ or Python.
3. Participants are requested to have an account on HackerRank.
4. Any offline IDE can be used but use of any online IDE other than the one provided by HackerRank is not allowed.
Team Distribution:
1. FE-SE (Junior Team): Team of 2
2. TE-BE (Senior Team): Team of 2
3. Individual
